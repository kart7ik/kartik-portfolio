04 — Attribution & Influencer Ecosystem

Every Rupee.
Tracked. Attributed. Accountable.

Influencer marketing has a measurement problem. We solved it by building a coupon-led attribution system that turned every collaboration into a measurable ROI line — making partnership decisions data-backed, not instinct-backed.

Introduced influencer-branded coupon codes as the primary tracking mechanism — each creator gets a unique code, every sale gets a clean attribution source.
Built lead-triggered discount flows — turning DM inquiries into conversion opportunities by arming the sales pipeline with dynamic offer mechanics.
Created a measurable ROI framework per influencer — revenue generated, code redemptions, and conversion rate tracked for every partnership at a glance.
Enabled data-driven partnership scaling — doubling down on high-performing creators and eliminating vanity collaborations that look impressive but deliver nothing.
Why This Matters
Most brands can't tell you which influencer actually drove revenue. We built the system that made that question answerable — turning a guesswork channel into a predictable growth lever.
